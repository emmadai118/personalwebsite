About me

Hi, I'm Emma (Ching Yu Dai).

I'm an architecture student and artist with a practice rooted in concept-driven making. My work begins with an idea — a feeling, a film, a historical text — and finds its form through model-building, painting, and drawing.

I'm drawn to the spaces between things: the tension between structure and fragility, the way scale shifts perception, the emotional weight that materials can carry. Whether I'm constructing a 50-inch architectural model or working in oil on canvas, the question is always the same — what does this space feel like from the inside?
